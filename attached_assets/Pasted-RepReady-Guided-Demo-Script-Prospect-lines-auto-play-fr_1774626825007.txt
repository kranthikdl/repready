RepReady — Guided Demo Script
Prospect lines auto-play from the app. Alex runs the full demo solo.

Setup — SDR (Alex) — Desktop + Browser
Open RepReady in a browser tab — https://rep-ready.replit.app/
On the setup screen:
Name → Alex
Call Type → Discovery
Coaching Priorities → select all three
Mode → Guided Demo
Click Start Session
Click Start Live Transcription — grant mic permission when prompted

The Call — Alex speaks these lines live into the mic. Prospect lines appear automatically.
Alex: Hi, thanks for taking the time today. I'm Alex from Velocity. How are you doing?
→ App plays: "Hey Alex, doing well! Busy quarter but happy to chat."
Alex: Totally get it. So I know you signed up after seeing our webinar on pipeline acceleration. I'd love to learn a bit more about what caught your eye there.
→ App plays: "Yeah, we've been struggling with our outbound process. Our SDR team is spending too much time on manual research and not enough time actually selling."
Alex: That makes sense. A lot of teams we work with face that exact challenge. So tell me, how many SDRs do you have on the team right now?
→ App plays: "We have about 12 SDRs. We hired aggressively last quarter but ramp time has been brutal. Most of them aren't hitting quota yet."
Alex: Got it. And what does your current tech stack look like for prospecting? Are you using any automation tools today?
→ App plays: "We have Salesforce and we use LinkedIn Sales Navigator. But honestly our process is pretty manual. Reps are copying and pasting a lot."
Alex: I see. Let me tell you about our platform then. Velocity automates the entire prospecting workflow. We integrate directly with Salesforce and can pull enriched data from over 50 sources.
→ App plays: "That sounds interesting. But I'm curious, how does it handle personalization? We don't want to send generic spray-and-pray messages."
Alex: Great question. Our AI engine analyzes each prospect's digital footprint and generates personalized talking points. Your reps get tailored suggestions without doing the research manually.
→ App plays: "Hmm, that's intriguing. We tried a similar tool last year though and the quality wasn't great. The messages felt robotic."
Alex: Yeah that's a common concern. Our approach is different because we don't write the messages for you. We surface the insights and your reps craft the message. It's augmentation, not automation.
→ App plays: "Okay, that distinction is helpful. What kind of results are your customers seeing?"
Alex: On average, teams see a 40% increase in meetings booked within the first 90 days. Our best customers have cut research time by 70%.
